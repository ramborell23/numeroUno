The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandiwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks gret when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A sharp knife
  • A butter knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread jelly on one slice of bread
  3. Spread peanut butter on the other slice of bread
  4. Combine both peices with the jelly and peanut butter facing each other
